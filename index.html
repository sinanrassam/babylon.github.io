About Us

Babylon Charitable Trust is a non-profit organization established in 2005 to provide community-based services for migrants of Middle Eastern origin within the Auckland region. Based in the North Shore of Auckland, we are registered with the New Zealand Companies Office and the Charities Commission (CC49388).

Our mission is to unite Middle Eastern families living in New Zealand, helping them preserve their culture and language while becoming caring, responsible, and knowledgeable citizens of their new homeland.

أهدافنا

Our Objectives

  • 1 To promote friendship, mutual respect and understanding between the Community and New Zealand society.
  • 2 To preserve, revive and disseminate cultural heritage of the Community among new generations.
  • 3 To develop charitable activities and welfare of the Community in New Zealand.
  • 4 To provide information and advice to assist new migrants of the Community to settle in New Zealand.
  • 5 To promote joint charitable activities and cooperation with New Zealand society through various cultural programmes.
  • 6 To participate actively in the development of a culturally diverse and charitably cohesive society in New Zealand.

Our Programmes

We offer three core programmes designed to serve different generations of our community, from youth education to seniors support.

60+ Years

Seniors Programme

برنامج كبار السن

Supporting our senior community members who help bind families together with their cultural values and wisdom.

  • Pick-up and drop-off transportation
  • English ESOL & Communications
  • Health checks (blood pressure, sugar)
  • Social gatherings & light activities
  • Outdoor activities & venue hire
